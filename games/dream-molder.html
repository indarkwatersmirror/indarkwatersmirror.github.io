Dream Molder Info:
A fan Visual Novel about a shy Braixen that tries her hardest to overcome her shyness to get her owner to take notice her feelings for him.

My first small game/novel project that I'm hoping to practice and grow with.
It's a piece of work and nothing professional but I'm hoping that it grows and improves together with me, becoming
something great and enjoyable for everyone in the end.

Story Summary:
In a region that is still in it's babysteps towards human and Pokémon living together, a young boy and his childhood friend
find a wounded and barely alive Fennekin.
Taking it home with them they nurse it back to health and discover a bond that goes beyond friendship.

When the boy suddenly has to move he leaves his two friends behind without goodbye, not hearing from them again until years later...

By now they've all grown up to be different people with different dreams and plans for their future.
The Fennekin evolved to a Braixen by now and needs a new place to stay.

And even though his very traditional family doesn't think much of the idea of human and Pokémon living together under the same roof the now young man gladly takes Braixen in.
What he doesn't know however is that his old friend and Braixen has a secret or two and some habbits that she rarely has control over...

A big shoutout to all my patrons over on Patreon, without I wouldn't be able to invest the time into this and improve at all. Thank you for your support.
